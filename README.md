## Professional Profile

I build web platforms, internal tools, CMS-oriented products and technical systems that are designed to be useful in production, not just visually attractive.

My work sits at the intersection of software development, infrastructure and product execution. Through AEWhite Devs, I ship websites, tools, changelog systems, business platforms and self-hosted services with a strong focus on clarity, maintainability and real-world utility.

## AEWhite Devs

| Area | What I build | Typical focus |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Web Development | Websites, portals, dashboards, CMS solutions and internal platforms | Product structure, UX clarity, performance and maintainability |
| Infrastructure and Hosting | Self-hosted services, deployment environments and operational tooling | Reliability, monitoring, backups, scalability and uptime |
| Automation | Practical utilities, release workflows and internal process tooling | Time savings, consistency and better day-to-day operations |

## Web Ecosystem

- [AEWhite Devs Portal](https://aewhitedevs.com/): the main company website that presents services, public products, portfolio direction and brand identity.
- [AEWhite Devs Herramientas](https://herramientas.aewhitedevs.com/): a public utility hub with developer and productivity tools such as timezone conversion, `.gitignore` generation, regex testing and SSH key generation.
- [AEWhite Devs Changelogs](https://changelogs.aewhitedevs.com/): a dedicated public release-notes system that tracks product updates, version history and deployment changes across projects.
- [AEWhite Devs Status](https://status.aewhitedevs.com/): a service-status endpoint for visibility into operational health and uptime.
- [AEWhite Devs Blog](https://blog.aewhitedevs.com/): the editorial and content side of the ecosystem, aligned with the AEWhite Devs platform.

## Visible Products and Platforms

- `MyPyme Control (Web)`: a business-oriented platform with a redesigned interface, responsive layout, dashboard widgets, dark and light mode support, performance improvements and stronger visual consistency.
- `MyPyme Control (Android)`: a mobile application rebuilt with Jetpack Compose and Material 3, including WebView-based navigation, native intents, file handling and in-app update support.
- `MyPyme Control (Desktop)`: a desktop application rebuilt on Tauri 2 with system tray behavior, auto-start options, persistent preferences and update management.
- `AEWhite Devs Herramientas`: a growing collection of public utilities designed for practical day-to-day use by developers, teams and technical users.
- `AEWhite Devs Changelogs`: a polished release-history platform that makes product evolution, fixes and feature delivery transparent.
